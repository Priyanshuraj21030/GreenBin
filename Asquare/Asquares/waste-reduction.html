Waste Reduction Guide

Simple steps to minimize waste in your daily life

Quick Tips for Waste Reduction

Use Reusable Bags

Replace plastic bags with reusable shopping bags to reduce plastic waste.

Start Composting

Turn food scraps and yard waste into valuable compost for your garden.

Buy in Bulk

Reduce packaging waste by purchasing items in bulk using reusable containers.

Step-by-Step Waste Reduction

1

Assess Your Waste

Track your household waste for a week to identify main sources of waste:

  • Keep a waste diary
  • Categorize different types of waste
  • Identify patterns and areas for improvement
2

Plan Your Shopping

Smart shopping habits to reduce waste:

  • Make a shopping list and stick to it
  • Choose products with minimal packaging
  • Bring reusable bags and containers
3

Kitchen Waste Reduction

Tips for reducing food waste:

  • Plan meals in advance
  • Store food properly to extend shelf life
  • Use leftovers creatively
  • Start a compost bin
