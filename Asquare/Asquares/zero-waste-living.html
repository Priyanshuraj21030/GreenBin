Zero Waste Shopping Guide

Before Shopping

  • Make a shopping list
  • Bring reusable bags
  • Take containers for bulk items
  • Check what you already have
Grocery Shopping
  • Shop at farmers markets
  • Buy from bulk bins
  • Choose package-free produce
  • Bring mesh produce bags
Personal Care
  • Look for plastic-free packaging
  • Choose refill stations
  • Buy in bulk when possible
  • Support eco-friendly brands
General Tips
  • Quality over quantity
  • Choose durable items
  • Avoid single-use products
  • Shop second-hand first
